# Online Resource 1

**Article title:** Oxygenation versus tissue composition in positronium-lifetime PET: an identifiability limit for hypoxia imaging

**Journal:** European Journal of Nuclear Medicine and Molecular Imaging

**Author:** Ernest Darell Zermeño

**Affiliations:** Universidad Panamericana, Zapopan, Mexico; Universidad de Guadalajara, Zapopan, Mexico

**Correspondence:** 0244552@up.edu.mx

Parametric Monte Carlo validation of the fixed-nuisance Poisson CRLB under the assumed spectrum model. Pulls of $\hat{p}O_2$ are close to $N(0,1)$, showing that the maximum-likelihood estimator reaches the fixed-nuisance CRLB when the forward model is correctly specified.
